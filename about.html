Our Story

Wriothesley Baked Goods was founded with a passion for baking and a dream to share delicious treats with the community. From a small kitchen to a full-fledged bakery, our journey has been fueled by love for fresh, homemade goods.

Meet Our Team

Our skilled, hard working bakers and our friendly staff work hard every day to bring you the best pastries, bread, and cakes for our customers to enjoy.

Our Mission

To create high-quality, freshly baked treats that bring happiness to our customers and support local farmers by using the best ingredients.
